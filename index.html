Who am I?

I am Australian, living in greater Melbourne. Right now I work in plant biosecurity and one of the projects here is related to that. Previously I have been an English language teacher and an infantryman in the Australian army. I have a life-long learning mentality - recently I discoverd coding and web development and have been learning ever since.

My coding journey

I started learning about web development while I was looking for an indoor pastime during the Covid lockdowns. I found I enjoyed creating web pages so In 2021 I completed a front end development course by Treehouse where I created 9 projects for assessment as well as passing an exam. From there, I have continued developing through the #100DaysOfCode challenge by building in public.
